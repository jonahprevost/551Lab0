Mountain Hunting the East Slopes of Alberta

Welcome to Mountain Hunting Alberta! The goal of this page is to educate and inform on the opportunities for the pursuit of mountain big game animals in the East Slopes of Alberta's Rocky Mountains. This website will dicuss hunting strategies, habitat, and current scientific data and hotspots for the three most common mountain big game species of Alberta.

Mountain Game Species

  • Rocky Mountain Bighorn Sheep
  • Rocky Mountain Mule Deer
  • Rocky Mountain Elk (Wapiti)

Population Statistics

Please note these harvest numbers are only for mountain units/ areas. Harvest of Mule Deer and Elk in non-mountain environments is substantial. These statistics are for males of the species only.

Species Mountain Harvest Numbers Percent of Total Harvest Distribution
Bighorn Sheep 141 100% Entire Front Range and Alpine Areas
Elk 144 3% Subalpine and Grassy Alpine Areas, Spotty Distibution
Mule Deer 153 2% Subalpine and Grassy Alpine Areas across Front Range
